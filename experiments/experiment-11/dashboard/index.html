Equipment owner deploys Husqvarna-style robots at three partner sites.

Policy has two sources:
INSTANT PAP-native — pushed to AuthzForce on every Create/Delete.
≤10 s ConsumerAuth → PIP → PAP sync — PIP polls ConsumerAuth, PAP background loop rebuilds XACML when PIP version changes.

Both sources are merged into a single XACML PolicySet. Deduplication ensures no duplicate rules when the same subject/resource appears in both.
