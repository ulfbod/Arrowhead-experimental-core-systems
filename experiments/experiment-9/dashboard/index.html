Experiment 9 — UC3 Lawn Mowing as a Service

Arrowhead 5.2 · XACML · mTLS · Multi-Site UC3
UC3 Scenario
Equipment owner deploys Husqvarna-style robots at three partner sites.

Robot Sites 1/2/3 publish IMU telemetry to Kafka (TLS) and AMQP (TLS).

Portal & Cloud ML subscribes to the Kafka stream via kafka-authz (XACML PEP) and aggregates all site data.

Service Partners SP1/SP2 consume the aggregated REST API via pki-rest-authz (mTLS + XACML PEP).

Policy decisions are made by AuthzForce using grants from ConsumerAuth compiled by policy-sync.
